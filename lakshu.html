Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue?

Simply fill out the report form with a clear title, description, and photo of the issue. Select the appropriate region and submit anonymously.

Is my report anonymous?

Yes, you can submit reports completely anonymously. We don't require personal information for basic reporting.

How long does it take to resolve an issue?

Resolution times vary depending on the issue type and location. Most reports are acknowledged within 24-48 hours.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report road accidents, damaged roads, streetlight outages, potholes, drainage issues, and other civic infrastructure problems.

How do I track my report?

While reports are anonymous, you can check the Upload a Report section to see if similar issues have been reported and their status.

Road Repair Initiative Completed in Delhi

January 10, 2026

The Department of Infrastructure Development has successfully completed a major road repair initiative across Delhi. Over 500 potholes were fixed and 20km of roads were resurfaced in the past month.

The project, which began in December 2025, addressed critical infrastructure issues reported through the Pocket Infrastructure platform. Citizens reported over 200 issues that were prioritized for immediate attention.

Smart Street Lighting System Launched

January 5, 2026

A new smart street lighting system has been implemented in residential areas. The system uses energy-efficient LED lights with automatic sensors that adjust brightness based on pedestrian activity.

Monsoon Drainage System Enhanced

December 28, 2025

Following multiple reports of waterlogging during the recent monsoon season, the drainage system in low-lying areas has been upgraded with larger capacity pipes and additional drainage outlets.
